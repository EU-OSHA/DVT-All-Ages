Historical Evolution - Older workers employment rates

Increasing the employment participation of older people is challenge for most European countries. Several European countries already record an increase of the employment rate of older workers and of the effective retirement age, however, participation rates are still low in many countries. Health is an important factor for leaving the labour market, but there are other factors influencing employment participation at a higher working age. The questions to answer are: Are older people able to work? Are they motivated to do so? Can they?
