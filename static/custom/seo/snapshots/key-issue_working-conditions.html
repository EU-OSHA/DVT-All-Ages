Dissatisfaction of older vs. all workers

Factors related to working conditions, such as lack of working time flexibility, exclusion from training, not viewed positively by employers, lack of skills, workplaces not adapted to the needs of people 55 and over (Eurobarometer 378) play an important role in people’s decision to stop working.

Find out how older workers perceive their working conditions and compare against some indicators of OSH management that are relevant in the context of an ageing workers.
